GAME IN DEVELOPMENT

"The Path Into The Abyss" is a first-person shooter where you will have to survive in an Argentina immersed in the apocalypse.

What is the abyss?

"The abyss" is the City of Buenos Aires, so called because it is ground zero, where the first arrival of the invaders that we will have to face in the game takes place.

dynamic NPCs

You will find characters in and around the abyss, each with a story to tell. You can make them your travel companions, turn them into your allies or even your enemies.

Ambientación

You will be able to explore the city of Buenos Aires, the forests of Ezeiza and its surroundings, each with its own ecosystem affected by the abyss.

Difficulty

The game aims for extreme difficulty and there is no map, so you will have to trust your instincts and navigate carefully.
